BizTrack Pro — Business Tracker (English)

A clean, powerful business management web app for small business owners — track sales, expenses, and profit with zero setup.

✨ Features <br>
💰 Sales & Revenue tracking — log every transaction<br>
📉 Expense categorization — know where money goes<br>
📦 Inventory management — stock in, stock out<br>
📊 Profit & Loss dashboard — visual, real-time<br>
📓 Business diary — notes and observations<br>
🌙 Dark & Light mode<br>
📱 Mobile-first design — works on any device<br>
💾 No account needed — all data stored locally<br>

💡 The Problem It Solves<br>
Most accounting software is overkill for small businesses. BizTrack Pro gives you 80% of the value in a single HTML file — no installation, no subscription, no complexity.<br>
